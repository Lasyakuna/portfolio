Project


(Landing Page)


To veiw the landing page, click on the link --> pilgrim's




Our aim was to design a Landing Page for any brand. So we chose a cosmetic brand named Pilgrim's. We divided the Landing Page into three different parts:

  1. Products available-In this part, we have mentioned about the products available and their description.
  2. Pricing-In this part, the MRP rates(including all taxes) for each product and the combo offers provided by the company is mentioned.
  3. Contact Us-In this part, if the buyer has any questions or need assistance they can contact the company by giving their details.
